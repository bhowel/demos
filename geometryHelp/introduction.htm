Introduction to Geometry

Geometry is the subject we use to measure, describe, and create space and objects in space. The word geometry comes from two Ancient Greek words: geo (earth) and metria (measurement). The modern definition reflects the original meaning of the word.

You can use geometry to figure out the shape or size of an object or to determine the location of one object in relation to another. Objects include points, lines, and shapes. Shapes can be flat, such as circles and triangles, or solid, such as spheres and cubes.
